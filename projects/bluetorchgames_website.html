Project Overview

        A small indie game studio passionate about creating fun, replayable experiences. We build our games with care—from pixel to program—and believe in gameplay-first design with strong visuals, compelling feedback loops, and mechanical depth.

Blue Torch Games is a compact development team crafting indie games with a strong gameplay core. Our team consists of:

🎮 Lead Programmer

🎨 Artist/Animator

🔧 Secondary Programmer

We emphasize originality, responsiveness, and player-driven design, aiming to make small games with big hooks.

Whether it’s a blocky adventure voxel game, a fishing idle clicker, or a dungeon crawler—you’ll find something unique in our lineup.
