Project Overview

        Battle through an endless dungeon with randomized floors, deadly traps, and hordes of monsters. Choose your class, explore different varients of hand crafted levels, and see how long you can survive. Steam demo out now!

        That One Dungeon is a fast-paced, infinite dungeon crawler made in Godot 4. The player selects a class and fights through never ending dungeon levels filled with enemies, traps, and treasure. The goal is to survive as long as possible, reaching a high score before death inevitably catches up. Every run is different, and each room may hide secrets that reveal the dungeon‚Äôs dark history.
