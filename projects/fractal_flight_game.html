Project Overview

        Float through a vibrant, generative sky of fractal clouds and balance your glider through a neon dreamworld. Explore a hypnotic blend of art and motion where every sky is uniquely formed.

        Fractal Flight is a minimalist flight experience created for the Gamedev.js Jam 2025 under the theme Balance. You pilot a paraglider across a procedurally generated fractal sky, balancing your movement through shifting psychedelic clouds above a glowing neon cityscape.

    Glider Mechanics:

  • A simple physics loop mimics gliding through the air using Three.js vector math.

    • Fractal Sky:

  • Procedurally rendered with fragment shaders or generated noise to build complex skies.

    • City Background:

  • A parallax scrolling image adds depth beneath the clouds.

    • Color Shifts:

  • The sky colors pulse and flow using time-based hue shifts.
